<h3>Proof of Identity (POI)</h3>
<div>
<p>The Australian Business Register (ABR) will check the personal details you enter here against the existing details on the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) systems. If these details don&apos;t match, then your application may need to be assessed manually or your application may be unsuccessful.</p>
<blockquote><hr /><strong>Please ensure</strong> your details with the ATO are up to date! To <a href="https://www.ato.gov.au/Business/Registration/Update-your-details/">update your tax details</a> visit the ATO website.<hr /></blockquote>   
<h4>Where can I find out more?</h4>
<p>Details on what information the ABR needs to <a href="https://abr.gov.au/General-information/Proving-your-identity/">prove your identity</a> can be found on the ABR website. </p>

<h3>Tax File Number (TFN)</h3>
<div>
<h4>Do I have to provide my TFN?</h4>
<p>If you&apos;re applying for an AUSkey, you <strong>must</strong> provide your TFN.</p>

<p>If you&apos;re <strong>not</strong> applying for an AUSkey, you <strong>don&apos;t</strong> have to provide your TFN. However, if you don&apos;t have a TFN or don&apos;t provide one, your application will be delayed by up to 28 days.</p>
<h4>Why the delay?</h4>
<p>Without a TFN, the Australian Business Register (ABR) may need to manually process your application. To do this, the ABR will need you to provide additional information or certified copies of documents including your: </p>
<ul><li>bank account number</li>
<li>birth certificate</li>
<li>passport</li>
<li>driver’s licence.</li></ul>
<h4>Can I provide a TFN later?</h4>
<p>If you don&apos;t have the TFN with you, you can leave the form and come back to it later. The form will be saved automatically.</p><p>As long as you don&apos;t lodge the form, you can return later and log in to resume your application from the Application summary screen. </p>
<h4>Where can I find out more?</h4>
<p>Details on what information the ABR needs to <a href="https://abr.gov.au/General-information/Proving-your-identity/">prove your identity</a> can be found on the ABR website. </p>
